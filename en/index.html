Water knowledge

Open hydrological science for better informed decisions.

Kimün-Ko brings together field observations, satellite products, atmospheric reanalyses and hydrological modelling to understand how water is changing across Chilean catchments.

Based at Universidad de La Frontera, the observatory turns those data into verifiable technical information, open tools and analysis that connects hydrological processes with real impacts on communities and territories.
